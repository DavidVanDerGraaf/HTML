About Cloud9

Cloud9 is a North American esports organisation currently fielding teams in Hearthstone, League of Legends, Smite, Super Smash Bros. Melee and Wii U, Heroes of the Storm, Halo, CS:GO, Overwatch, Rainbow Six, PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds, Fortnite, and Rocket League. This page will be about their CS:GO teams.
